Energetica

Exhibition

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica is coming to the Community Science Museum. It’s a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them. From solar energy powering lighting to ‘Wind Island’ that shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power.

Cosmology

Exhibition

Our fantastic exhibition, ‘The Sky Above Us’, explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. Follow the journey of our solar exploration: from early Arab traders, to Galileo’s telescope, to the latest exploration of the planets in our solar system.
