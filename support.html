Support Us

The Community Science Museum relies on support from a wide range of sources and there are many ways that you can donate to help care for and preserve the collection for future generations.

Our Mission

The Community Science Museum exist as a result of generosity of our donors, sponsors and members. Your contributions will help preserve our collections and support daily maintainance. The support is crucial for us to develop new contents for our visitors and continue our activities to inspire the future generation. There are several ways that you can extend support to the museum. Check out the options below and get in touch if you have other ideas to help.
