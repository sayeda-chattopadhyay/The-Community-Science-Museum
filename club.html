About the Club

The Young Stars Club is an activity club designed for children and youth who can explore and enjoy the beauty of science. Our expert team give the children guided tours of the museum, teach them in our learning laboratory, and provide great video presentations that will excite and inspire them. The children get to experience the great joy of exploring and doing things together. The children develop new skills and expand their knowlegde. The club helps kids make friends and practice social skills with both adults and their peers. They experience the joy of creating something new and exciting which they can take home. The Young Stars Club is suitable for children from 3rd to 7th grade. There are special events during the school holidays.

Holiday Club

During the school holidays we run special holiday clubs where you can join other children of your age to go on a journey of discovery. During each holiday we pick a new theme to explore. For the upcoming summer holiday 2022 we will explore on the topics of global warmimg, paleontology and ancient Egyptian history, among other things.
